FAQ

    How do I open a UK National Bank Account?

    UK National Bank Account must be opened in person or it can be opened by authorizing a trusted person to do it for you.

    UK National Bank Account in British Pounds or in foreign currencies?

    UK National Bank Account are in British Pounds except for Sterling Services Accounts which are in Pound Sterling. ATM Cards can be utilized to obtain local currencies where applicable. Local currencies and checks can also be purchased at your Community Bank banking center.

    What types of checking accounts are offered by UK National Bank ?

    Checking accounts include Regular Checking, Interest Bearing Checking.  For more information on all products, contact your Community Bank representative.

    What are the requirements to open a UK National Bank Account?

    To be eligible for full banking services at UK National Bank, you must meet at least one of the requirements below:
    Your full name, home address, home telephone number, your campus telephone number, and your college address (Letterhead showing the local address – i.e. utility bills or apartment contract)
    •Unexpired passport
    •I-94 card
    •I-20, DS-2019 or I-797 approval notice
    • Any secondary form of identification (such as: your passport, your Student ID card, birth certificate, state driver’s license, or a letter from the International Students and Scholars Office)
    •Enrollment verification letter from your school

    Are Savings Accounts offered by UK National Bank ?

    Savings Accounts are available through UK National Bank offering competitive interest rates with interest compounded and credited monthly. For more information on all products, contact your Community Bank representative.

    What types of Overdraft Protection does UK National Bank offer?

    Overdraft Protection can be linked to your Regular Checking or Interest Bearing Checking to provide you extra protection against overdrafts or returned checks. A Line of Credit is a revolving line of credit linked to your Regular Checking or Interest Bearing Checking to provide overdraft protection when needed. Credit is subject to approval. For more information contact your Community Bank representative.

    Can my local bills be paid automatically?

    You can request that your rent and other regular local bills, such as utility and telephone bills, be paid from your Checking or Sterling Accounts by contacting your local Community Bank banking center representative to find out about availability in your location.

    I am deploying, can UK National Bank Credit help me to ensure my bills are paid?

    Contact your local UK National Banking center to discuss your pending deployment and concerns or click here for online resources.

    Does UK National Bank Credit provide ATM Cards?

    Yes. You may choose to receive a UK National Bank ATM Card to access your account through Free man Trust ATMs or thousands of AFFN® or CIRRUS® network locations around the world. You may also use your Community Bank ATM card at some point–of–sale terminals. Contact your UK National Bank representative for more information.

    How can I get my account information when the local banking center is closed?

    UK National Bank offers several ways to get your account information when your local banking center is closed.
    •With Free man Trust Online Banking, you can access your accounts from your computer 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
    •Additionally, Interactive Banking / Bank by Phone gives you access to your accounts from almost anywhere, anytime. Contact your local Ghana International Bank to establish your PIN (Personal Identification Number) to get started

    I recently tried to use Interactive Banking / Bank by Phone to get information about my account, I was asked for my PIN (Personal Identification Number). I am not sure I have one. How can I get one, or verify that I have one?

    Contact your UK National Bank or Customer Service representative during normal banking hours for information on how to utilize Interactive Banking / Bank by Phone.

    How can I get information about loans?

    Contact your UK National Bank to find out about the various financing options available for new/used cars and motorcycles, Signature loans and/or CD secured loans. See our Loan Products page for additional information.

    How can I place a stop payment on a check?

    Timing is important in stop payment situations. Contact your UK National Banking center or Customer Service representative to place a stop payment order during normal local banking hours.

    How do I make a wire transfer?

    Contact your UK National Bank center or Customer Service representative during normal banking hours for information on how to make a wire transfer.

    How do I change my UK National Bank Account address? Contact your UK National Bank center or Customer Service

    Contact your UK National Bank center or Customer Service representative during normal banking hours for information on how to change your account address

    My overseas assignment has changed and I must relocate to another country, does this impact my accounts?

    Due to governmental regulations when your assignment requires relocation to another country, depending on the country location, you may need to close your existing UK National Bank Account and open another International Bank account. For more information, contact your Trust Micro Credit center representative.

    How do I close my UK National Bank account?

    Contact your UK National Bank or Customer Service representative during normal banking hours for information on how to close your account
